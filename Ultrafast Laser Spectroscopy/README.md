This Ultrafast Laser Spectroscopy Folder contains the data and analysis files used by Nicholas Willen and Riccardo Rollo to conduct experiments with in their second semester of their junior year (Spring 2026) at Harvard. They used pump and proble pulses to excite quantum particles into higher energy states then observing them before they have the time to return to ground state, which takes like a few femtoseconds (10^(-15) seconds). This was used to study phonons in bismuth and antimony

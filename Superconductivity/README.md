This Superconductivity Folder contains the data and analysis files used by Nicholas Willen and Riccardo Rollo to conduct experiments with superconductors in their second semester of their junior year (Spring 2026) at Harvard. They studied the resistivity drop in metal samples at a critical temperature. They studied the diamagnetism in superconductors, finding the critical fields of different elemental metals and alloys and produced Meissner graphs.
